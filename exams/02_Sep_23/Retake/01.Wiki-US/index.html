The United States of America (USA), is a country
primarily located in North America and consisting of 50
states, a federal district, five major unincorporated
territories, and nine Minor Outlying Islands. It includes 326
Indian reservations. It is the world's third-largest country by
both land and total area.

It shares land borders with Canada to its north and with
Mexico to its south and has maritime borders with the
Bahamas, Cuba, Russia, and other nations. The national
capital of the United States is Washington, D.C., and its most
populous city and principal financial center is New York City.
